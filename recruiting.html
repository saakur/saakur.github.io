Open Undergraduate Student Positions


If you are an undergraduate student already at Auburn University and are interested in working with me, send me an email or drop by my office hours to chat! I am always happy to mentor students interested in computer vision and artificial intelligence.

Prerequisites: You should have a strong background in programming, preferably in Python and taken COMP 3270.

Open Ph.D. Student Positions


We are always looking for motivated PhD students, especially in embodied active perception, multimodal event understanding, and computer vision applications for agriculture and medicine. Several of our current and former students have published at top venues in computer vision and machine learning such as CVPR, ECCV, WACV, MICCAI, etc.

Our Ph.D. program is light on coursework (only 6 courses required post-baccalaureate, and none if you have a Masters in CS). This is designed for students to focus on research. PhD Students at Auburn are typically funded with full tuition fees and stipend (as either RA or TA). You can learn about Auburn’s Computer Science and Software Engineering PhD program here.

If you are interested in applying for a Ph.D. position with me, drop me an email with the following information:
  • Your latest CV.
  • Any courses that you have taken that is relevant to research in AI (e.g. statistics, math, machine learning, etc.)
  • A research statement (1-page max) about what excites you most about research and what topic you want to work on. Include information about your possible start dates and what you expect to get out of this Ph.D. program.
  • Any other material that you feel is relevant to support your application (e.g. videos of your projects; code samples).
Preferred qualifications: A strong mathematical background and programming skills. Relevant experience with the lab's overall goals..

About Auburn University and the City


Auburn is recognized as one of the “best small towns in America,” with moderate climate and easy access to major cities and to beach and mountain recreational facilities. Situated along the rapidly developing I-85 corridor between Atlanta (GA) and Montgomery (AL), the combined Auburn-Opelika-Columbus statistical area has a population of over 500,000. Auburn has been previously ranked by CNNMoney, USNews, Forbes and recently Livability as one of the best places to live in the U.S.

Auburn University (AU) is a public research university and the flagship university in the state of Alabama. AU ranked #40 among public universities. The computer science and software engineering (CSSE) department is the highest ranked department in Alabama, fourth among SEC schools, a top-50 department among public universities, and among the top 15% of all departments in the nation according to the latest rankings by USNews. The graduate CS program at Auburn is ranked #89.

In addition to being an R1 Carnegie research institution, Auburn Engineering research is ranked among the nation’s top 50 in research expenditures.
